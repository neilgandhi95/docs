## Understanding all metrics and signals available

### **Who opened it**

- Name
- Email
- Phone
- Location

### How engaged they were

- Time spent
- Number of opens
- Which parts did they read  (for PDF files only)

### **Live activity**

Watch in real-time as they go through your stuff

### Instant alerts: 

Get pinged the second someone opens your content
